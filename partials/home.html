Chemical Damage - Metabolic In Silico Network Expansion Databases

Welcome to the CD-MINE database. This database contains spontaneous reactions that occur under physiological conditions curated from literature as well as reactions predicted by generalized reaction rules. There are many ways to explore this data and if it's your first time on the website, we recommend taking one of the tours below. You can also explore the reactions and reaction rules built within this database using our dynamic tables. See the Tutorial videos item in the top menu-bar for more detailed documentation of the CD-MINE and the other MINE websites.
